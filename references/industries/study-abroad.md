# 出国留学 / 留学 Playbook

Use with `references/industry-module-framework.md`. This module merges the duplicated C-column rows for `出国留学` and `留学`.

Source: merged from row 11 `出国留学行业观点情绪选题生成器` and row 30 `留学行业观点情绪选题生成器`.

## Aliases

- 出国留学
- 留学
- 海外升学
- 国际教育
- 留学申请

## Use When

- The request is about overseas study applications, school selection, major selection, visa concerns, family budget, parent-child decision conflict, agency trust, or overseas adaptation.
- The offer is an international education, study-abroad consulting, application planning, visa guidance, or overseas-life support service.

## Do Not Use When

- Use `education-training.md` for general education courses or training not tied to overseas study.
- Use `college-employment-placement.md` for domestic job placement, internships, resumes, interviews, or就业安置.
- Use `college-students.md` for broad campus life or student identity content.

## Audience

Primary audience: 学生、家长、留学申请家庭、国际教育咨询用户.

## Core Emotional Tension

教育跃迁期待、路径选择焦虑、长期投入压力和海外不确定性并存.

## Trust Barriers

Key barriers: 机构资质、案例真实性、费用、签证、学校/专业匹配、海外适应成本.
